about

As of fall 2016, I am a NASA Earth and Space Science fellow in the final year of my PhD at University of Michigan, studying with Brian Arbic. I grew up near Acadia National Park in Maine, and have a great appreciation for the outdoors. I completed my undergraduate studies in physics at Kalamazoo College, where I also studied math and studio art and had the opportunity to study abroad in Strasbourg, France. I enjoy reading, sailing, snowboarding, exploring new places, and eating new foods.
