about

As of fall 2017, I am a post doctoral scholar at Scripps Institution of Oceanography working with Jen MacKinnon, Amy Waterhouse, and Sam Kelly. During my tenure at Scripps, I am studying an internal tidal beam in the Tasman Sea, using data collected during a joint experiment in 2015 called T-TIDE. I finished my PhD as a NASA Earth and Space Science fellow at University of Michigan, studying with Brian Arbic. I grew up near Acadia National Park in Maine, and have a great appreciation for the outdoors. I completed my undergraduate studies in physics at Kalamazoo College, where I also studied math and studio art and had the opportunity to study abroad in Strasbourg, France. I enjoy reading, sailing, snowboarding, exploring new places, and eating new foods.
